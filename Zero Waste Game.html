About the game

Learn about Phoenix's recycling programs in a fun way with our brand new Zero Waste sorting game. Sort materials correctly and win fun items to build your own digital park!

On behalf of the City of Phoenix, ReCollect Systems has launched a web analytics program that is used to record non-identifiable information about your game visit. The data collected is aggregated and is not personally identifiable. IP addresses are masked so that they cannot be used to identify individuals. Our web analytics will also respect any “do not track” settings you might have set on your browser.
